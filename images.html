Phil Ochs Photo Gallery

This is a collection of images of Phil Ochs from a variety of sources. Many thanks to iag@cis.ufl.edu (Isaac Alden Green) for contributing most of these images. Further contributions are welcome.

  • Mark S. Millman was at Phil's concert in Vancouver, B.C. March 15, 1969 and took a bunch of pictures. (this is the concert on There and Now - Live in Vancouver 1968)
  • The Phil Ochs Memorial Page has several photos taken at a rally in Military Park in Indianapolis in May of 1971, along with an image of a newspaper clipping about Phil's death.
