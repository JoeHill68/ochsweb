Related performers

This is an attempt to list performers considered (by someone) to be related in some way to Phil Ochs. It's far from compleat. Please contribute any names/links. Also, links to other relevant web pages can be found here

Some general resources which may be useful are Folkmusic.org, FolkLib Index, The AllMusic Guide, The Sixties web page (lots of related links) and another Sixties web page. Also, The Museum of the City of San Francisco has a Rock chronology which mentions Phil.

Also Hard Miles Music

Let me know if you know of any other info which should go here.

Predecessors/Influences

  • Joe Hill. Some of his songs can be found in Digital Tradition Of course, Phil wrote a song about him.
  • Woody Guthrie. Phil also wrote a song about Woody.
  • Pete Seeger
  • The Beats

Contemporaries

  • Jac Holzman (who produced Phil's early albums)
  • Elvis
  • Bob Gibson could also be listed as an influence/predecesor. Several of Phil's early songs used Bob Gibson's melodies, and many others clearly show Gibson's influence. Some web pages are (finally!) starting to pop up, here's a Discography
  • Jim & Jean. Jim Glover introduced Phil to Folk music (Woody Guthrie, Pete Seeger, &c.) and he and Phil performed together as the Sundowners during their college days. Jim & Jean put out a couple of albums which contained several of Phil's songs, most notably Changes and Crucifixion.
  • Bob Dylan
  • Joan Baez
  • Tom Paxton
  • Dave Van Ronk
  • Victor Jara
  • Eric Andersen
  • Arlo Guthrie (warning: this web page is nearly unusable from text based browsers, but it has lots of eye-candy for the rest of you)
  • Harry Chapin
  • Carolyn Hester
  • Van Dyke Parks
  • Jesse Colin Young

Successors/Influencees

Many of these people/groups performed at the recent Phil Ochs nights (organized by Sonny Ochs).

  • David Rovics
  • Jim Page, here's an interview, and another page
  • Hugh Blumenfeld. His album Mr Jekyll & Dr Hyde is dedicated to Phil Ochs.
  • Dar Williams She wrote a song mentioning Phil called All My Heroes Are Dead. IMHO, she is one of the best songwriters around these days.
  • John Wesley Harding. He wrote a song mentioning Phil: Phil Ochs, Bob Dylan, Steve Goodman, David Blue and Me, and did a great cover of Another Age on the recent tribute album
  • Josh Joplin. He wrote a song about Phil and did a cover of Half A Century High.
  • Sid Griffin did a cover of Sailors and Soldiers on the recent tribute album
  • Ani DiFranco has recorded a cover of When I'm Gone
  • Billy Bragg
  • John Dachik
  • Phil Cohen
