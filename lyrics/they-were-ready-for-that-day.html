They Were Ready for That Day

By Phil Ochs

Oh they came from the roads, and they came from the sky,
and they all came together, some to sing, and some to cry,
fate was strong, and their hopes were high,
they were ready for that day

And a feeling of glory was shared by every man,

and 200,000 were all standing there
gone was the waiting, and gone was the fear
